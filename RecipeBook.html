    • Recipe #1 - Healthy Cheetos

    • Time: 30mins
    • Serves: 5 people

    Instructions:Sound the alarms: There is a home-baked alternative to Cheetos. Do you know what that means? Guiltless, cheesy, crunchy snacking. Or mostly guiltless, cheesy, crunchy snacking. Sure, you have to make them yourself. And yes, that means you have to get your butt into the kitchen. But, it's possible. And they're delicious.

    Ingredients:
    4 tbsp. chilled unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch cubes.
    1/2 tsp. kosher salt.
    garlic powder.
    1 c. unbleached all-purpose flour.
    yellow cornmeal.
    4 oz. ounces Monterey Jack cheese, finely shredded.
